FUEN President

"Anyone travelling through the Nordic landscape quickly recognizes a distinctive scene: countless islands scattered across the sea, standing alone yet connected by the water that surrounds them..."

Dear guests, dear members, dear delegates,

As President of the Federal Union of European Nationalities, I am delighted to welcome you to our 2026 Congress in Helsingfors • Helsinki.
Our strength lies above all in cooperation. When one minority makes progress, the hopes of others grow too. When rights come under pressure, a local challenge becomes a European task.
The protection of our languages, cultures and identities, as well as greater European responsibility for minority rights, remain central concerns of our work.
